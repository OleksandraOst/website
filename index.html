The Sunyaev-Zeldovich effect in galaxy clusters

For my Master's research project, I analyze images from Atacama Large Millimeter Array (ALMA telescope) to study the properties of gas between galaxies (so-called intracluster medium) with the help of the Sunyaev-Zeldovich effect.

Searching for chaotic behaviour in blazars

Blazar variability appears to be stochastic in nature. However, a possibility of low-dimensional chaos was considered in the past, but with no unambiguous detection so far. If present, it would constrain the emission mechanism by suggesting an underlying dynamical system.

Science-Policy Research

Another research project which I'm deeply interested in is the protection of dark and quiet skies for astronomy and mankind. I work on light and atmospheric pollution from satellites, and space sustainability. Also, I am an affiliated member of the IAU-CPS Policy Hub.
