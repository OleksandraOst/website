Time Series Analysis

Time Series shows data changes over time. I was working on time series data of active black holes (blazars). Blazar is a type of active black hole (which is located in the center of the galaxy and emits the flow of particles pointed directly towards Earth). In this analysis, I also used the theory of chaos to describe the behavior of the blazars.

LLM utilizations

I created an AI-power tool (Ainfer) that quickly extracts key information from articles and accurately answers complex questions. Designed to streamline the research process, Ainfer allows users to summarize articles and work with multiple files simultaneously. Ainfer identifies the most relevant parts of an article and provides reliable answers. With Cohere's multilingual embeddings, Ainfer can search for answers in over 100 languages, making it a powerful tool for audiences worldwide. Ainfer was created with Streamlit, offering a visually appealing and user-friendly interface for customers. Whether it's for academic research or just personal knowledge-seeking, Ainfer is the ultimate information extraction tool.
