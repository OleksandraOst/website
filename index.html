Searching for chaotic behaviour in blazars

Blazar variability appears to be stochastic in nature. However, a possibility of low-dimensional chaos was considered in the past, but with no unambiguous detection so far. If present, it would constrain the emission mechanism by suggesting an underlying dynamical system.

Ainfer

Ainfer is an innovative AI solution that transforms the way students and researchers access information. Equipped with cutting-edge Cohere technology, Ainfer quickly extracts key information from articles and delivers accurate answers to complex questions. Designed to streamline the research process, Ainfer allows users to summarize articles and work with multiple files simultaneously. By leveraging Cohere's advanced embedding model, Ainfer identifies the most relevant parts of an article and uses Cohere's state-of-the-art generative model to process the information and provide reliable answers. With Cohere's multilingual embeddings, Ainfer can search for answers in over 100 languages, making it a powerful tool for audience worldwide. Ainfer was created with Streamlit, offering a visually appealing and user-friendly interface for customers. Whether it's for academic research or just personal knowledge-seeking, Ainfer is the ultimate information extraction tool.

Satellite Debris Tracking

Space junk accumulates over time and is harmful to satellites and space missions. Our graphical user interface allows users to track all known debris and satellites in real-time, as an educational part of project we provide more information about objects with references to NASA and other open sources. We present an interactive application that allows users to track all known debris and satellites in real-time, providing information and educational materials from NASA and other open sources. In addition, we applied a machine learning algorithm to predict future coordinates of debris and potentially reduce the risk of collisions. Our app specializes in tracking debris and visualizing their trajectories. We consider adding historical statistics and improving our ML model as potential next steps.
